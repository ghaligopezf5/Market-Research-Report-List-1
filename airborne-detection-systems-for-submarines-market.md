<p><p>Airborne Detection Systems for submarines consist of various technologies designed to enhance anti-submarine warfare capabilities. Sonobuoys are buoyant devices dropped from aircraft that detect underwater sounds. Dipping sonars are deployed from helicopters or maritime aircraft, allowing for detailed underwater scanning while hovering. Radars, primarily used for surface detection, can also assist in identifying submarine periscopes or features above water. Other technologies include magnetic anomaly detectors and acoustic sensors, which collectively improve situational awareness and target tracking in naval operations.</p></p>
