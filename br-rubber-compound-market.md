<p><p>The BR Rubber Compound market consists of different types, primarily focusing on CIIR (Chlorinated Isobutylene-Isoprene Rubber) and BIIR (Brominated Isobutylene-Isoprene Rubber). CIIR is known for its excellent heat resistance and good aging properties, making it suitable for various automotive and industrial applications. On the other hand, BIIR offers enhanced ozone and weather resistance, making it ideal for outdoor applications. Both compounds are valued for their versatility and performance in manufacturing tires, hoses, and other rubber products.</p></p>
