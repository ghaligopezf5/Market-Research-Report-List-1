<p><ul><li>Mechanical Concrete Anchors</li><li>Adhesive Concrete Anchors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The concrete anchors market is primarily divided into two types: mechanical concrete anchors and adhesive concrete anchors. Mechanical concrete anchors rely on physical expansion within the concrete to create a strong hold, making them suitable for high-load applications and immediate use. Adhesive concrete anchors, on the other hand, use a bonding agent to secure the anchor in place, providing strong resistance to environmental factors and suitable for heavier loads with a longer curing time. Both types serve various construction and engineering needs.</p></p>
