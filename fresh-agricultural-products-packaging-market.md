<p><p>The Fresh Agricultural Products Packaging market comprises several key types. Modified Atmosphere Packaging (MAP) enhances shelf life by altering the gas composition inside the package, slowing decay. Vacuum Skin Packaging (VSP) tightly seals products, removing air to preserve freshness and appearance. Vacuum Thermoformed Packaging (VTP) uses heat to shape packaging, creating a snug fit that protects items. Other packaging types include traditional methods like shrink wrap and biodegradable options, catering to diverse consumer preferences and sustainability concerns.</p></p>
