<p><p>Hyperbaric Oxygen Therapy (HBOT) is a medical treatment that involves inhaling pure oxygen in a pressurized environment. The therapy operates in specialized chambers where pressure is increased beyond normal atmospheric levels, facilitating oxygen delivery to tissues.</p><p>The HBOT market is projected to experience significant growth, with an expected CAGR of 4.3% during the forecast period.
