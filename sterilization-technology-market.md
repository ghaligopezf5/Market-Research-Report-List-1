<p><p>The sterilization technology market is primarily divided into two types: chemical and physical sterilization. Chemical sterilization utilizes agents like ethylene oxide and hydrogen peroxide to eliminate microorganisms, making it ideal for heat-sensitive medical instruments. Physical sterilization, on the other hand, employs methods such as steam, dry heat, and radiation to achieve sterility. Each method has distinct applications in healthcare and pharmaceuticals, ensuring the safety and effectiveness of medical products by effectively eradicating pathogens.</p></p>
