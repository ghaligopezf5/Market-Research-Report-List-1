<p><strong>The PTFE Membrane Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hydrophobic PTFE Membrane</li><li>Hydrophilic PTFE Membrane</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PTFE membrane market is categorized into hydrophobic, hydrophilic, and others. Hydrophobic PTFE membranes repel water and are primarily used in filtration applications, ensuring gas permeability while blocking liquid particles. Hydrophilic PTFE membranes, conversely, allow water to pass through while filtering out contaminants, making them ideal for biological and medical applications. The "others" category encompasses various specialized membranes tailored for unique industrial needs, such as chemical resistance and durability in extreme conditions, broadening the applications of PTFE technology.</p></p>
