<p><p>Mobile Network Testing can be categorized into two main types: indoor testing and outdoor testing. Indoor testing focuses on measuring coverage, signal strength, and data speeds within buildings, where obstacles like walls may affect network performance. It often includes assessments of various facilities such as offices and malls. Conversely, outdoor testing evaluates network performance in open environments, such as cities and rural areas, considering factors like terrain, weather conditions, and user mobility, ensuring optimal connectivity for users on the go.</p></p>
