<p><ul><li>Single Frequency Locator</li><li>Multi-Frequency Locator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The underground cable locator market is primarily divided into two types: single frequency locators and multi-frequency locators. Single frequency locators emit one fixed frequency to detect utilities, making them simple and cost-effective for straightforward applications. In contrast, multi-frequency locators can operate at various frequencies, enhancing their ability to identify multiple types of underground utilities and providing greater accuracy in complex environments. This versatility makes them suitable for more demanding tasks, such as locating cables in congested areas.</p></p>
