<p><p>Nitrile gloves are available in two primary types: powdered and powder-free. Powdered nitrile gloves are coated with a mild powder, typically cornstarch, which makes them easier to don and doff, particularly in situations where hands may be damp. However, they can cause irritation for some users. Powder-free nitrile gloves are treated to eliminate the powder, making them suitable for sensitive skin environments, such as medical applications or food handling, where contamination or allergic reactions are a concern.</p></p>
