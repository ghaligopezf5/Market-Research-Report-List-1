<p><p>The radiation detection, monitoring, and safety market encompasses various technologies used to measure ionizing radiation. Geiger counters are widely used for their simplicity and effectiveness in detecting gamma and beta radiation. Scintillation detectors utilize scintillating materials to convert radiation into light, providing high sensitivity and resolution. Solid-state detectors, such as semiconductor devices, offer excellent energy resolution and compact designs, suitable for various applications. Other technologies include ionization chambers and personal dosimeters, catering to specific detection needs across industries.</p></p>
