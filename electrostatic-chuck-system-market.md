<p><strong>The Electrostatic Chuck System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Colulomb Type Electrostatic Chucks</li><li>Johnsen-Rahbek(JR) Type Electrostatic Chucks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electrostatic Chuck System market encompasses primarily two types: Coulomb Type and Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type Electrostatic Chucks. Coulomb Type Electrostatic Chucks utilize electrostatic forces generated from applied voltages to hold wafers in place during semiconductor manufacturing processes. In contrast, JR Type Electrostatic Chucks capitalize on a dielectric layer to enhance adhesion, enabling higher holding forces and better thermal stability. Both types are integral in improving manufacturing efficiency and precision in the semiconductor and electronics industries.</p></p>
