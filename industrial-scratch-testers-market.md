<p><p>The industrial scratch testers market comprises three key types: </p><p>1. Ordinary Scratch Testers evaluate material resistance to surface scratches using a single-point contact method. </p><p>2. Linear Scratch Testers simulate real-world wear by employing a controlled linear motion, assessing durability more accurately over a defined path. </p><p>3. Multi Finger Scratch Testers use multiple points of contact to replicate the effects of various environmental factors, providing a comprehensive assessment of material resilience against scratches and wear. Each type serves distinct testing requirements in material evaluation.</p></p>
