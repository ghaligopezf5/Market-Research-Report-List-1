<p><strong>The Inertial Sensing Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Attitude Heading and Reference Systems (AHRS)</li><li>Inertial Navigation Systems (INSs)</li><li>Inertial Measurement Units (IMUs)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The inertial sensing systems market encompasses three primary types: Attitude Heading and Reference Systems (AHRS), Inertial Navigation Systems (INSs), and Inertial Measurement Units (IMUs). AHRS provide orientation data, crucial for aircraft and marine applications, while INSs combine navigation data to ascertain position and velocity, essential for both civilian and military uses. IMUs measure acceleration and angular velocity, serving as vital components in various applications, including robotics, automotive, and aerospace, enabling precise motion tracking and control.</p></p>
