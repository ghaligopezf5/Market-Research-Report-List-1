<p><p>Die bonding paste adhesive is categorized into two main types: conductive and non-conductive. Conductive adhesives facilitate electrical connections between components, making them essential in applications requiring efficient signal transmission and thermal management, such as in semiconductors. Non-conductive adhesives, on the other hand, provide strong bonding without electrical conductivity, suitable for insulative applications where electrical isolation is needed. Both types cater to various industrial sectors, including electronics, automotive, and aerospace, depending on specific performance requirements.</p></p>
