<p><ul><li>Vacuum Coolant Filter Paper</li><li>Gravity Coolant Filter Paper</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The coolant filter paper market is primarily segmented into two types: vacuum coolant filter paper and gravity coolant filter paper. Vacuum coolant filter paper is designed for high-efficiency filtration, using suction to draw coolant through the paper, effectively removing contaminants and enhancing the performance of machining processes. In contrast, gravity coolant filter paper relies on gravitational force, allowing coolant to pass through the paper naturally. Both types serve essential roles in maintaining the quality and longevity of metalworking fluids in various industrial applications.</p></p>
