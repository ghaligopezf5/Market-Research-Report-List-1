<p><p>The digital resistivity meters market comprises two main types: surface resistivity meters and volume resistivity meters. Surface resistivity meters measure the resistivity of materials at the surface level, often used in applications like concrete testing and monitoring in construction. In contrast, volume resistivity meters assess the resistivity of a material throughout its volume, making them ideal for applications in materials science and soil analysis. Both types serve critical roles in evaluating electrical properties in various industries, including construction, geophysics, and environmental studies.</p></p>
