<p><p>The superheated water sterilizer market includes two primary types: shower sterilizers and spray sterilizers. Shower sterilizers utilize high-temperature water vapor directed onto surfaces to effectively eliminate pathogens, making them ideal for large areas and equipment. In contrast, spray sterilizers disperse superheated water through fine mist, allowing for targeted disinfection in hard-to-reach places. Both types are favored in various industries, including healthcare and food processing, due to their efficiency and eco-friendly nature, promoting a cleaner and safer environment.</p></p>
