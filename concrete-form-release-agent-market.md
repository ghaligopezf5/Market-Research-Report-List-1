<p><strong>The Concrete Form Release Agent Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Internal Mold Releases</li><li>External Mold Releases</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The concrete form release agent market is divided into two main types: internal mold releases and external mold releases. Internal mold releases are incorporated into the concrete mix, enhancing the ease of demolding and reducing surface imperfections. They are often used in precast applications for a smoother finish. External mold releases are applied to the mold surface before pouring concrete, creating a barrier that prevents adhesion. Both types improve product quality and efficiency in concrete manufacturing processes. </p></p>
