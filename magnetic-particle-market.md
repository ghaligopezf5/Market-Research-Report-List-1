<p><p>The magnetic particle market primarily consists of two key types: ferrites and neodymium-iron-boron (NdFeB). In contrast, NdFeB magnets are known for their high magnetic strength and are utilized in advanced applications such as electric motors, speakers, and various industrial sectors. Both types play distinct roles in driving innovation and efficiency in different markets.</p></p>
