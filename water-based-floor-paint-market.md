<p><strong>The Water-based Floor Paint Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water-Diluted Paint</li><li>Water Dispersible Paint</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The water-based floor paint market encompasses two main types: water-diluted paint and water-dispersible paint. Water-diluted paint is mixed with water before application, allowing for easier cleanup and reduced emissions of harmful solvents, making it environmentally friendly. In contrast, water-dispersible paint contains pigments and resins dispersed in water, providing excellent adhesion and durability. Both types are celebrated for their ease of use, quick drying times, and vibrant finishes, making them popular choices for residential and commercial flooring applications.</p></p>
