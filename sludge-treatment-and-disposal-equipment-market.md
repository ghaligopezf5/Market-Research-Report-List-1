<p><p>The sludge treatment and disposal equipment market comprises several types, including screw presses, centrifuges, belt filter presses, and other technologies. Screw presses efficiently dewater sludge through mechanical compaction. Centrifuges utilize high-speed rotation to separate solids from liquids, enhancing dewatering effectiveness. Belt filter presses employ a continuous belt system to filter and extract water, ensuring consistent sludge treatment. Other equipment types may include polymer dosing systems and rotary drum thickener technologies, catering to diverse industrial and municipal wastewater treatment needs.</p></p>
