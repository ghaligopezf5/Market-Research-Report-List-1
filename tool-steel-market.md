<p><p>The tool steel market comprises three main types: carbon tool steel, alloy tool steel, and high-speed tool steel. Carbon tool steel, characterized by its high carbon content, offers good hardness and wear resistance, making it suitable for cutting tools. Alloy tool steel includes additional elements to enhance strength and toughness, ideal for heavy-duty applications. High-speed tool steel is designed to withstand high temperatures generated during cutting, allowing for faster machining speeds, making it essential in manufacturing cutting tools and dies.</p></p>
