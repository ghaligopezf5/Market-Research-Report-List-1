<p><p>The thermal ablation devices market comprises various types, including interstitial probes, which deliver heat directly into tissues for targeted treatment. Grounded probes utilize conductive methods to destroy undesirable cells. Needle applications involve inserting a needle to ablate tissues through heat. Radiofrequency ablation uses electromagnetic waves to generate heat and disrupt cellular integrity. Hydrothermal ablation employs heated fluids to achieve tissue destruction, while microwave ablation harnesses microwave energy to achieve higher temperatures for effective ablation of tissues.</p></p>
