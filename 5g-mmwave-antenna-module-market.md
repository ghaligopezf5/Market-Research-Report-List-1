<p><p>The 5G mmWave antenna module market primarily includes two key types: phased array antenna modules and RF transceiver antenna modules. Phased array antenna modules utilize multiple antennas to steer beams electronically, enhancing signal quality and coverage, essential for high-frequency 5G applications. In contrast, RF transceiver antenna modules integrate both transmitting and receiving functions, optimizing communication efficiency. Together, these modules enable the high-speed, low-latency performance critical for 5G networks, supporting advanced applications in various sectors like telecommunications and IoT.</p></p>
