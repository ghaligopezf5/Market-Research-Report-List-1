<p><p>The household plastic bottle market encompasses various types of materials, each serving unique purposes. Polyethylene (PE) is widely used for its durability and moisture resistance, often seen in detergent bottles. Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene (ABS) offers strength and impact resistance, suitable for various applications. Polyethylene Terephthalate (PET) is commonly used for beverage bottles due to its clarity and recycling potential. Other materials also contribute to specialized applications in this versatile market.</p></p>
