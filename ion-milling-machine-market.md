<p><p>The ion milling machine market is categorized into two main types: cross-section milling and flat surface milling. Cross-section milling is used for precision preparation of samples, allowing for detailed analysis of internal structures by removing material at selected angles. Flat surface milling focuses on creating smooth, even surfaces on samples, ensuring uniform thickness and quality. Both types enable enhanced examination and characterization in materials science, semiconductor research, and nanotechnology, driven by demand for high-precision manufacturing and sample preparation.</p></p>
