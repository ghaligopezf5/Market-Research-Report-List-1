<p><p>The saliva collection devices market is categorized into two main types: whole saliva collection devices and glandular saliva collection devices. Whole saliva collection devices are designed to gather mixed saliva from the mouth, providing a comprehensive sample that reflects overall salivary composition. In contrast, glandular saliva collection devices specifically target saliva from individual salivary glands, allowing for more focused analysis. These distinctions cater to varying diagnostic needs, influencing applications in healthcare, research, and diagnostics across diverse fields.</p></p>
