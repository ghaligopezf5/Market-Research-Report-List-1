<p><p>The flexographic printing self-adhesive label market comprises two main types: release liner labels and liner-less labels. Release liner labels utilize a backing paper that facilitates easy peeling and application, ideal for intricate designs and multi-layered formats. In contrast, liner-less labels eliminate the backing, allowing for more eco-friendly solutions and space-saving designs, which can enhance efficiency in high-volume applications. Both types cater to various industries, including food, beverages, and cosmetics, offering versatile labeling solutions for diverse packaging needs.</p></p>
