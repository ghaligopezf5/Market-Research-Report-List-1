<p><ul><li>Hollow Shaft</li><li>Solid Shaft</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The helical gearmotors market is primarily segmented into hollow shaft and solid shaft types. Hollow shaft gearmotors feature a central opening, allowing for easier mounting on existing equipment and accommodating larger shafts for torque transfer. In contrast, solid shaft gearmotors have a continuous solid structure, providing robust support and stability during operation. Both types cater to various industrial applications, with hollow shafts often used in space-constrained environments, while solid shafts are preferred for heavy-duty applications requiring greater durability.</p></p>
